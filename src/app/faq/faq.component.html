Frequently Asked Questions


Returns

                   
How do I return my purchase?
                   
If you would like to return an item, please click on the return link located at the bottom of the page. Input your order number and select the item that you would like to return. Once you have selected the item, click create a return ticket. you will be emailed a return label so you can ship the item back to us.        
                           
How do I return a gift sent to me?
                   
If you would like to return a gift, you can contact us by clicking the contact us link at the bottom of the page. In the "whats on your mind" section box, please tell us you are returning a gift. We will create a shipping label so you can ship the item back to us.        
                           
How do I retrieve a misplaced label?
                   
If your label has been lost or misplaced, you can click the contact us link at the bottom of the page and tell us what happened. Please include your order number or other reason for return and we will replace the lost label for you.        
                      
How long does it take for me to get my return?
                
We will work fast to get your return processed as quickly as possible! Our returns normally process between 2 to 3 business days.       
          

General

                   
What countries is shipping offered in?
                   
We currently ship anywhere in the United States.        
                           
How do I customize a kit?
                   
Some of our kits allow you to pick colors for certain items. When you choose the kit that you would like to purchase, you will have the option to choose between different colors. However, you cannot add or delete items from kits.        
                           
How do I purchase individual items?
                   
We currently do not offer the ability to buy individual items, we only sell the items as part of a kit.        
                      
How can I write a review on a product?
                
You can click on the "Write a review" link that is located below the kit on the kit page.       
                      
How do I unsubscribe from your mailing list?
                   
If you would no longer like to be a part of our mailing list, you can click the "unsubscribe from mailing list" located at the bottom of any email newsletter we have sent you. You can also send us a message from the "contact us" page and request to be taken off of our mailing list.        
                       
How do I update my account information?
                   
To update your account information, click the "my account" tab located on the menu bar. From there, you can click account information, to bring you to your account page to update all of your information.        
           

Payment Information

  • Mastercard
  • American Express
  • Discover
  • JCB
       
                           
Can I pay with multiple cards?
                   
Sorry, we currently only allow you to pay with a single card.
