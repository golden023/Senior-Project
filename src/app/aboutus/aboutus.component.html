Why EveryDay Kits?

We know that planning for all sorts of different events can be quite a hassle. You have to make a list of everything you need and hope you do not forget anything. That is why we made EveryDay Kits. Everday Kits allows you to buy kits for parties, camping, hurricaines, and more! Everything you need for your event will be included in your kit that you purchase. No more making a list and having to go to the store, just go online and look for the kit you need and order.
